KIT PAULSON IS COLOSSAL
Wearable Glass Objects & Sculptures / 10.28.17
GLASS ARTIST Kit Paulson has a broad interest in art, science, and history that intersects superbly in her career as a glass sculptor. Over the past several years she’s explored human anatomy through a variety of skull and bones pieces, as well as wearable objects like masks, armor, and even a pair of gloves.
Paulson is currently finishing an MFA at Southern Illinois University and will be teaching upcoming workshops at Pittsburgh Glass Center, Bildwerk Frauenau, and Pilchuck Glass School.

You can follow her most recent work on Instagram.
